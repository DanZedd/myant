PACE goes high tech with smart clothing that monitors your heart health.
 
 

Dear Katherine Elphick,

As an alternative to traditional ECGs, I'm excited to announce that PACE Cardiology will soon be offering cardiac testing with washable smart clothing that uses tiny fibres to track your heart rate, ECG, temperature and more.

This groundbreaking personalized healthcare project is in partnership with Myant: the company behind the Skiin family of bio-sensing garments. Licensed by Health Canada, these easy-to-wear garments feature textile electrodes knitted into the fabric to help us monitor your heart health and general wellness.

In the near future, PACE can utilize this same technology to replace cumbersome holter monitors.

Known as the Myant Virtual Clinic, the goal of this program is to bring convenient, high quality remote care to my patients. Not only does the program allow me to better monitor your health, it keeps you and your care circle engaged in the process.

Warm Regards,
Dr. Yaariv Khaykin.
 
 
 
How does it work?
 

These comfortable, yet high-tech garments have sensors knitted into the fabric that detect your vital signs, such as your heart rate and core body temperature.

A small, removable pod sits in the garment's band, which sends this information to the Skiin Connected Life app on your smartphone, where you can view and share it with myself and/or your loved ones.

The garments are machine washable and the pod battery can last up to 3.5 days on a full charge.
 
 
 
 
What are the benefits?
 

Because the Skiin Connected Life app allows you to share your data with me, I can proactively track your health metrics and ECG data should you have an abnormal rhythm, palpitations or other symptoms related to your heart.

The app also has a diary feature that lets you track daily symptoms over time. As a Health Canada approved Class II medical device, you may be able to receive reimbursement through your benefits provider.

The garment kit costs $275 CDN and includes 2 garments, the pod, charger and access to the app.
